Biography

A native of Ireland, Madeleine, has enjoyed teaching 2nd languages in different school settings - private high schools, public schools and higher education - across the world. She has taught Irish, French and Music to all ages. She taught computer classes to adult learners in the evening while managing the home with three small children. She has lived abroad and learned first hand the difficulties of learning a 2nd language and adjusting to a 2nd culture. Madeleine emigrated to the USA in the early 90's and despite having fluent English, a bachelors degree in her pocket, still struggled with cultural adjustment that is involved with moving to a new home. Madeleine has always taught - even when setting up her own school as a child in the playroom at home - lecturing to a series of dolls and teddy bears with a chalk board. She now lives in New England with her husband and four children, dogs, fish, cats and a lizard.
